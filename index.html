An Artist's Showcase

The website I made for Frederick R McDonald

Frederick R McDonald is an international, award-winning artist: painter, poet and photographer who lives in Fort McMurray, Alberta (Canada). Fred predominantly sells his paintings directly to collectors and he wanted a website to showcase his new artworks as well as his impressive career.

UX is the foundation of any site and I started this one with secondary research into the buying behaviour of art collectors. From there I structured the information architecture, followed by wireframes. I also crafted the copy, guided by the research. The fully customised coding began with the smartphone version first, using semantic HTML5, and I achieved the tablet and desktop layouts using the flexbox and CSS grid frameworks.

The site design provides a preview of his new artworks, just ahead of an exhibition. It highlights the artworks that are held in prominent art collections as an illustration of his importance in the Canadian art world. The site also includes several previously unpublished poems. Fred is an established cultural leader and this site draws attention to his many achievements in the worlds of art and publishing, academically and in his community.
