For a Great Customer Experience on Multiple Screen Sizes

Hi! I'm Mireille and I make websites. I use the latest coding frameworks - Flexbox and CSS Grid - to make websites that work on multiple Screen sizes, starting with the increasing important smartphone view. I'm also well-versed in the cutting-edge tools and techniques of user-experience (UX) to ensure your online customers have a great, user-friendly experience.

Making has always been my thing - whether it's art, clothing, jewellery or natural body products. I have even been known to make my own wild-craft herbal medicines and grow my own food. I love to cook - and help others have the necessities to cook (by volunteering at a foodbank). I've even worked on fishing boats - testing an investion made to prevent porpoise by-catch and keep the local inshore fishery open. I understand the unique challenges faced by makers and producers.
